What is LuxPDF?

LuxPDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, LuxPDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our PDF to PNG tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Convert as many PDF pages to PNG as you need without any cost or restrictions.
  • Privacy First: Your files are processed directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available, so you can verify our privacy claims and even contribute to the project.
  • High-Quality Output: Get crisp, high-resolution PNG images from your PDF pages, perfect for any use.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without the need to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • Flexible Download Options: Download all converted pages as a convenient ZIP file or select and download individual PNGs.

How to use the PDF to PNG tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your PDF file onto the upload area, or click to select it from your device.
  2. Step 2: Choose your preferred download option: a single ZIP file or individual page downloads.
  3. Step 3: Click the "Process Files" button to start the conversion.
  4. Step 4: Once processed, download your new PNG image(s) from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it safe to convert my PDF to PNG here?

Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser, so your files never leave your device. We do not upload or store any of your documents, ensuring complete privacy.

What is the quality of the output PNG images?

Our tool converts PDF pages to high-resolution PNG images, preserving the clarity and detail of your original document.

Can I convert a password-protected PDF?

For security reasons, our tool cannot process password-protected or encrypted PDF files. Please remove the protection before uploading.
