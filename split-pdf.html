What is LuxPDF?

LuxPDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, LuxPDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our Split PDF tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Split as many PDFs as you need without any cost or restrictions.
  • Privacy First: Your files are processed directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available, so you can verify our privacy claims and even contribute to the project.
  • Flexible Splitting: Split every page into a separate PDF or extract custom page ranges into new documents.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without the need to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • Batch Processing: The results are bundled into a convenient ZIP file for easy download.

How to use the Split PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your PDF file onto the upload area, or click to select it.
  2. Step 2: Choose your split method. Select 'Split by pages' to create a separate PDF for each page, or 'Split by range' to define custom ranges.
  3. Step 3: If using ranges, specify them in the input box (e.g., 1-3, 5, 8-10). Each range will become a new PDF.
  4. Step 4: Click the "Process Files" button to begin splitting.
  5. Step 5: Download your new PDF files, which will be provided in a ZIP archive.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How will I receive my files after splitting?

When you split a PDF into multiple new documents, all the resulting files are automatically packaged into a single ZIP file for a convenient, one-click download.

Does splitting a PDF affect its quality?

No, splitting a PDF is a lossless process. The content, layout, and quality of the pages in the new files will be identical to the original document.

Is it safe to split a confidential PDF with this tool?

Yes. The entire splitting process runs locally in your web browser. Your files are never sent to or stored on our servers, ensuring your data remains completely private and secure.
