What is LuxPDF?

LuxPDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, LuxPDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our Remove PDF Metadata tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Clean metadata from as many PDFs as you need, without any cost or restrictions.
  • Enhance Your Privacy: Remove all identifying information, such as author, title, creation date, and software used to create the file.
  • Privacy First: Your files are processed directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available, so you can verify our privacy claims and even contribute to the project.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without the need to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • Batch Processing: Remove metadata from multiple PDF files at once.

How to use the Remove PDF Metadata tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your PDF files onto the upload area, or click to select them from your device.

  3. Step 3: Once processed, download your cleaned PDF file(s) from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it safe to remove metadata from my PDFs here?

Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser, so your files never leave your device. We do not upload or store any of your documents, ensuring complete privacy.

What kind of metadata is removed?

Our tool removes all standard metadata fields, including Author, Title, Subject, Keywords, Creator, Producer, and Creation/Modification dates. This helps protect your privacy by stripping potentially sensitive information.

Will this change the content of my PDF?

No, the tool only removes the metadata. The visible content of your PDF, such as text and images, will remain completely unchanged.
