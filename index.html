Our Story & Philosphy

As college students, whenever we needed to submit homeworks or projects and needed to quickly convert our PDF Files for submission, we found that no service online fit our needs. They all charged high prices for the most basic services, were slow, had terrible UI, and worst of all, had access to view whatever we submitted to convert. We hated this concept of having to fork our privacy to corporations in order to convert our files.

Which is why we created LuxPDF, a file conversion and modification website that the does basic things better than everyone else. While everyone forces you to register, pay a subscription, holds everything in the cloud to view & access, and offer a terrible UI experience, we focused primarily on two things: Privacy, and User Experience. So we got to work, creating a service that not was extremely light-weight and efficient, but also completely Free and Privacy-Focused. We do not collect any data ever on the file you upload or modify using our service, as it was intended to be.

You might be wondering why, how, or what is the philosphy of LuxPDF. Why is it Free & Unlimited? Why no Registrations? How do you afford running the server? We want to be completely transparent with the way we plan and want to run LuxPDF. Our service, will be completely Free, with No Registration Needed, and Unlimited. We allow this simply because all conversions and modifications are done on your computer, client-side, meaning we never touch or view your files ever. This has benefits because it means we get to keep our website and codebase lightweight and fast offering a service that is faster and cheaper than all of our competitors. Our plan for affording servers and hosting is simply through Ads & Donations. We get it, privacy-focused but offering ads is a deal-breaker. However, in order to operate our service, we needed to decide whether or not to monetize through Ads or Paid Subscriptions. We believe that simple 2-3 Non-invasive Ads would be more beneficial for the end-user while allowing us to afford hosting and other services. We really want to provide a Ad-Free Expereince, but unfortunatly we can not afford it at this time. We happily take donations through BuyMeACoffee to help support us financially.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service Free?

Yes, LuxPDF is open source, free, with unlimited usage.

How is my privacy protected?

Your privacy is our top priority. All file processing happens directly in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security. We have zero access to your documents. You can verify this via inspect-element, that we never collect anything.

Why do you have ads?

As college students, it is difficult to afford not only student loans, but operating a website completely for Free. We believe that having a few ads and not charging subscriptions (and collecting sensitive information such as email, password details) is a better alternative for the general user.

What's the point of LuxPDF?

We wanted to create the first, and only PDF File Conversion & Modification website in the world that is not only completely free, unlimited, and open-sourced, but also focused entirely on efficiency, and privacy. We want to make the best service for basic File Conversion and Modification services in the world.

Is there a file size limit?

Theoretically, all processing happens on your computer, we never touch, convert, or modify your file. We technically support unlimited File Size and File amount, however, this is entirely dependent on your device's specifications and whether or not it can handle the size and amount of your requests. Generally speaking you can convert files upto 100MB with little to no issue.

How are you funded?

LuxPDF is funded through our minimally invasive Ads in order to pay for server hosting, domains, etc. We do take donations via BuyMeACoffee, which is always greatly appreciated.
