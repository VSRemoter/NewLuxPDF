Our Story & Philosphy

As college students, we constantly faced the frustrating challenge of converting & modifying PDF files for homeworks, projects, and other files with sensitive information such as names, financial information and more. We quickly realized that existing PDF-Toolkits just didn't cut it. They all demanded sign-ups and subscriptions for basic functions, were painfully slow, had clunky user interfaces, and, worst of all, compromised our privacy by allowing them access to our sensitive documents. The idea of sacrificing our privacy to corporate entities just to convert a file was unacceptable.

This frustration fueled the creation of LuxPDF: a PDF-Toolkit webapp designed to do the basics better than everyone else. While others forced you to register, pay subscriptions, store your data in the cloud, and offer subpar user experiences, we've laser-focused on two core principles: Privacy and User Experience. So we got to work, developing a service that's not only incredibly lightweight and efficient but also completely free and privacy-focused. At LuxPDF, we never collect any data on the files you upload or modify. Your documents stay on your device, just as they should. A crazy concept other services can't grasp.

You might be wondering: "Why is LuxPDF free and unlimited? Why no registrations? How do you cover server costs? What is your incentive?" We believe in complete transparency about our philosophy and how we operate. LuxPDF is, and always will be, completely free, with no registration needed, and truly unlimited. Because we crafted LuxPDF to have all file conversions and modifications performed directly on your computer, client-side, your files never leave your device and we never touch, view, or store them. This client-side processing also has significant benefits for you: it allows us to maintain a lightweight, fast, and efficient codebase, offering a service that's faster, cheaper, and more secure than our competitors. We strongly believe that there is no other service out there that is as privacy-focused as LuxPDF while being completely free to use, unrestricted, with no registration needed.

Our plan for affording server and hosting costs is straightforward: sponsorships, and voluntary donations. No trackers & data harvesting ads. All we're asking simply is, if you value what we do, we warmly accept donations via BuyMeACoffee to help support our mission. Any donators/sponsors will have their names/banner and a custom message of their choice listed on the webapp, as a Thank You.

-LuxPDF Admin Team

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LuxPDF truly Free?

Yes. LuxPDF is completely free to use with unlimited usage. There are no hidden fees, sign ups, or premium features hidden behind a paywall.

How is my privacy protected?

Your privacy is our highest priority. All file processing, conversion, and modification happen directly within your web browser (client-side). This means your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security. We have zero access to your documents at any point. For full transparency, you can use your browser's inspect element feature, or view our Privacy Policy to verify that no file data is ever collected or sent to our servers.

Why does LuxPDF have Sponsors & BuyMeACoffee?

As college students, we face the challenges of covering operational costs while trying to provide a completely free service. To avoid charging subscriptions (which would require collecting personal information like emails, and names), we've opted for a small number of sponsorship banners and public funding. We genuinely believe this is a far better and more respecting alternative for our users, allowing us to keep LuxPDF free and accessible to everyone. No sign ups, no premium paywalls, no data-collecting ads. We greatly appreciate your understanding and support.

What's the point of LuxPDF?

Our mission is to build the world's first — and only — completely free, unlimited, and open-source PDF conversion and modification platform that prioritizes efficiency and unwavering privacy. We're dedicated to offering the absolute best service for all your essential PDF needs.

Is there a file size or batch processing limit?

Because all processing happens directly on your computer (client-side), we never touch, convert, or store your files on our servers. This means we technically support huge file sizes & batch processing quantities. However, the practical limit largely depends on your device's specifications and its ability to handle large requests. Generally, you can convert several files up to 100MB with little to no issue depending on the operation.

How is LuxPDF funded?

LuxPDF is primarily funded through our minimally sponsors and donators, which help us cover essential operational costs like server hosting and fees. We also gratefully accept donations via BuyMeACoffee, and any support is always deeply appreciated as we strive to keep LuxPDF free for everyone.
